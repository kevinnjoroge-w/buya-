About Buya Musiq

Buya Musiq is an emerging Kenyan artist and producer making waves in the African music scene with a diverse catalog, including singles, EPs, and acoustic sessions.
Joseph Buya Muhia AKA Buya Musiq is a singer-songwriter, performing musical artist, and a music producer from Malindi, Kenya. He is well equipped in Afro fusion and Rhumba.
His music is accessible across major streaming platforms, and he maintains an active presence on social media for fans to follow his journey Buya Musiq is a talented artist known for his unique sound and captivating music. Explore his albums and follow him on social media to stay updated.

Background

Buya Musiq is based in Kenya (notably Chuka/Malindi), and is recognized for his legal background (LLB) as well as his music career.
His work often features collaborations with other African artists and explores themes relevant to his audience.

Notable releases

  • Milele (EP, 2025): Features tracks like "Milele" and showcases Buya Musiq's signature style.
  • Bheba (Single, 2025): An energetic dancehall track released in April 2025.
  • Rhumba Ya Wakili (Single, 2024): A collaboration with Vecrema, highlighting Buya Musiq’s versatility.
  • Glory (Single, 2024): Collaboration with $yok$, available on Apple Music.
  • Acoustic Sessions (2025): Presents stripped-down versions of Buya Musiq’s popular tracks
